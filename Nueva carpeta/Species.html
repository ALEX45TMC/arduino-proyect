Species at Risk

Explore the incredible diversity of marine life and learn about the species facing the greatest threats. Discover the challenges they encounter and the conservation initiatives dedicated to their protection.

Critically Endangered

Vaquita

The world's smallest cetacean, with fewer than 20 individuals remaining.

Hawksbill Sea Turtle

Known for its beautiful shell, this turtle faces threats from habitat loss and illegal trade.

Bluefin Tuna

A highly prized fish, overfishing has severely depleted its populations.

Endangered

North Atlantic Right Whale

Fewer than 400 individuals remain, threatened by ship strikes and entanglement in fishing gear.

Polar Bear

Melting sea ice due to climate change is rapidly destroying their habitat.

Great White Shark

Despite their fearsome reputation, these sharks are vulnerable to overfishing and habitat degradation.

Conservation Efforts

Numerous organizations are working tirelessly to protect marine species. These efforts include establishing marine protected areas, promoting sustainable fishing practices, combating pollution, and raising public awareness. Your support can make a difference in ensuring the survival of these magnificent creatures.
